Anyways, this soup must contain mushroom that you've foraged yourself. Otherwise your not allowed to make this soup. It also contains cream, garlic and a bit of parsley, which you are allowed to buy at the store.

Ingredients

  • 5 oyster-mushrooms
  • Around 1 bucket of thick cream
  • A bit of vegetable broth
  • One filled glass of white wine
  • A fistful of parsley
  • More garlic than you think
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking time

  1. Chop up all ingredients into medium sized pieces.
  2. Fry garlic on high heat.
  3. Add mushrooms right before the garlic turns black.
  4. Make sure to fry the mushrooms at least 15 minutes. Do not care if your food burns.
  5. Add wine and cream, let simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Add salt and pepper after taste.
  7. Garnish with parsley.
